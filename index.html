Ratha

A Sirenian refugee with the water spirit of a jellyfish, Ratha's expression is often blank, as though she isn't there. This sometimes comes off as clueless--other times, it seems erratic, even dangerous. Is she hostile, or friendly? Happy, or upset? It can be hard to tell until her behavior reveals the answer.

She isn't averse to conversation, however, and the wide, sharp-toothed grin that suddenly spans her face at times reminds one that she is, indeed, in possession of emotion. It is merely difficult to know when, or why, such emotion will manifest.

As with most Sirenia, she has a special emphasis on water magics, but has difficulty adapting to land-based combat.
